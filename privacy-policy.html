Privacy Policy for Chess Master

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

Introduction

Chess Master ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our chess game application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile information you provide when creating an account
  • Game Statistics: Your game results, win/loss records, difficulty preferences, and gameplay statistics
  • Profile Data: Age, country, and other optional profile information you choose to share

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: Game sessions, features used, and app performance data
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers (for crash reporting and analytics)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the chess game service
  • Track your game progress and statistics
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Communicate with you about the app
  • Ensure account security

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted database provider
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Your personal data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We do not store your password in plain text

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and user authentication
  • Google OAuth: For optional Google sign-in (if you choose to use it)
  • Expo: For app development and distribution platform

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your game statistics
  • Opt out of data collection (though this may limit app functionality)

Children's Privacy

Chess Master is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Game statistics are kept to provide historical gameplay data
  • You can delete your account at any time, which will remove all associated data
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes

International Users

If you are located outside your app's primary region, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" above.
